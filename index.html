Welcome to Uki-Stepping stone to tech life

It is a full scholarship based Accelerator program for Coding in Jaffna Sri Lanka targeting student who has completed Advanced Level examinations and don't have access to university education or any other form of vocational education. It is aimed to provide the necessary training to enter Computer Software industry or to start an IT startup This is a joint initiative by YARL IT Hub and SERVE Foundation Yarl IT Hub has been functioning for the last five years as a not for profit social enterprise focusing on technology entrepreneurship in northern Sri Lanka ans SERVE Foundation is a four year old Foundation focusing on using tecjnology to uplift secondary education standards in rural school in nothern Sri Lanka.
